ABOUT US

Welcome to RecipeShare, where culinary passion meets digital innovation. Immerse yourself in a world of flavors as we invite you to share and explore an array of delightful food recipes. From traditional classics to cutting-edge creations, our platform is your canvas to showcase your gastronomic expertise. Join a vibrant community of food enthusiasts who understand the magic that happens when ingredients harmonize. Elevate your cooking skills, indulge in culinary diversity, and create connections that revolve around a shared love for food. Get ready to embark on a journey that tantalizes taste buds and ignites creativity.

Founded in 2023 Recipeshare changed the food world by providing the tools to share recipes and cooking tips, while celebrating the expertise of home cooks online. Since then, Allrecipes has become the world's largest community-driven food brand, providing trusted resources to more than 60 million home cooks each month. Every day, cooks from around the world publish recipes and inspire one another through recipe photos, ratings, reviews, and videos. The combination of the Allrecipes community with our team of editorial and kitchen professionals provides authority found nowhere else on the internet and has turned the brand into an indispensable resource for cooks of all skill levels.

Social media influencers, registered dietitians, grillmasters, and more seasoned home cooks make up our enthusiastic squad of 100+ brand ambassadors. This diverse, food-loving crew spans the U.S. geographically and represents many different cultures, ethnicities, and family makeups. Since 2011, the Allstars have created tens of thousands of original recipes, photos, and reviews plus shared their cooking expertise via flat and video content on our website, social media, plus more marketing channels.
